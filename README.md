# APEX-SUPERMARKET-ANALYSIS
An interactive Power BI dashboard analysing sales performance across three supermarket branches.
📊 Project Overview
This dashboard presents an end-to-end sales analysis of Apex Supermarket across three branches (A, B, C) located in Yangon, Mandalay, and Naypyitaw. The goal was to uncover revenue trends, customer behaviour, and branch performance to support data-driven business decisions.

🔍 Key Findings

💰 Total revenue of $322,970 was generated across 1,000 transactions
🏆 Branch C (Naypyitaw) was the top-performing branch in both revenue and customer satisfaction
🛍️ Electronic Accessories was the top-selling product overall
📅 Saturday recorded the highest sales traffic across the week
💳 Ewallet was the most popular payment method, closely followed by Cash and Credit Card
⭐ Average customer rating across all branches was 6.97 / 10
